Transtones prides itself on providing the highest quality translucent resin panels products the world has to offer. It is our continuous goal to provide a level of service that is unmatched in the translucent stone industry. Having been in the industry for over 9 years, we've seen companies and businesses with similar products come and go, with Transtones you'll get a respected small business with many years of experience.

We are committed to be positive & aggressive in our attitude towards quality and service. We will not allow quality to take second place behind cost or schedule as this is our basic strategy for growth, it has helped us grow into one of the most highly regarded suppliers of translucent stone.

Our products are offered at fair prices, with an emphasis on working closely with our valued customers. We're also proud of all the great customer design photos in our portfolios, along with the wonderful testimonials they've sent us as well, it helps remind us of the importance of our commitment to providing the best products and the best service.

Transtones resin panels can be easily repaired and refinished back to its original condition, it can also be re-cut and reinstalled as a new design or reworked into new products such as bar counters, tables, kitchen tops, wall and ceiling and so forth.
